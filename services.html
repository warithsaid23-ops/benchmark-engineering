Our Disciplines

Six Sectors.
One Standard.

Government infrastructure to luxury private residences — every Benchmark project is held to the same international delivery standard.

What We Build

Comprehensive Construction
Capability Under One Roof

Most contractors in East Africa operate as coordinators — managing networks of subcontractors and passing work along. Benchmark is different. We own our plant fleet, employ our operatives directly, and manage every trade in-house.

That integrated model delivers three things that clients consistently tell us matter most: tighter programme control, clearer accountability, and consistently higher quality than you get when work is fragmented across multiple firms.

Below are the six construction disciplines in which we hold demonstrated, project-proven capability.

50+ Projects across all six disciplines
14+ Years of continuous delivery
Class 1 ZCR — maximum contractor classification

01

Building &
Civil Works

The foundation of our capability. Benchmark delivers reinforced concrete structures, masonry buildings, road formation, drainage systems, and the full range of civil engineering works that every major project demands.

Our Class 1 ZCR registration covers all building and civil work categories. Our plant fleet — Komatsu loaders, tracked excavators, compactors, and concrete mixers — means we are never dependent on third-party equipment to meet programme milestones.

Scope Includes

  • Reinforced concrete structures — foundations, columns, slabs
  • Masonry and blockwork construction
  • Road formation, sub-base and surfacing
  • Drainage, sewerage and utilities
  • Earthworks, excavation and ground stabilisation
  • Retaining walls and hard landscaping
Discuss a Building Project

02

Healthcare
Construction

Healthcare buildings demand a level of specification that most contractors in this region cannot meet. Benchmark can. Our portfolio includes Micheweni District Hospital on Pemba Island — a TZS 4.8 billion facility now serving 123,379 people.

Clinical environments require different thinking: infection control specification, patient-flow planning, clinical services co-ordination, and the kind of quality assurance documentation that satisfies Ministry of Health requirements. We deliver all of it.

Scope Includes

  • District and regional hospital construction
  • Clinical ward and theatre fit-out
  • Medical gas and specialist services installation co-ordination
  • Government procurement compliance (ZPPDA)
  • Health facility renovation and extension
See Micheweni Hospital

03

Education &
Government Works

As a ZPPDA-registered contractor, Benchmark has delivered government procurement projects since 2013. We understand the documentation requirements, the reporting obligations, and the stakeholder management that public-sector contracts demand.

Our education portfolio spans primary schools to secondary facilities across Unguja and Pemba. Our government works include ZSSF-funded residential and social infrastructure schemes. Benchmark treats public money with the same discipline we apply to private client funds.

Scope Includes

  • Primary and secondary school construction
  • Government office and civic buildings
  • ZSSF and social housing schemes
  • Public infrastructure and community facilities
  • Government procurement (ZPPDA) tender management
Government & Healthcare Sector

04

Luxury
Residential

Zanzibar's residential market is evolving rapidly. International buyers, diaspora investors, and discerning local clients expect a standard of finish that was previously unavailable on the islands. Benchmark delivers it.

Al-Saadi Apartments is our landmark residential scheme — a multi-storey development in Zanzibar City completed to a specification that required precision formwork, high-grade finishing, and a programme discipline that kept investors informed at every stage through our digital reporting system.

Scope Includes

  • Multi-storey apartment developments
  • Private villas and luxury residential compounds
  • High-specification internal fit-out
  • Swimming pools, terraces and external works
  • Bespoke joinery and architectural feature elements
Luxury Residential Sector

05

Commercial &
Hospitality

Zanzibar's hospitality economy is one of the fastest-growing in sub-Saharan Africa. Hotels, resorts, and commercial properties require a contractor who can meet international operator standards — not just a builder who can put up walls.

Benchmark's commercial portfolio includes hotel construction and renovation on both Unguja and Pemba, commercial retail fit-out, and office developments. Our digital oversight system gives hospitality clients the live reporting they expect when investment returns depend on programme certainty.

Scope Includes

  • Hotel construction and major renovation
  • Resort and leisure facilities
  • Commercial office and retail fit-out
  • Restaurant and food & beverage premises
  • Warehousing and light industrial buildings
Discuss a Commercial Project

06

Renovation &
Fit-Out

Renovation and fit-out work demands a different kind of precision. Working in occupied or partially occupied buildings, meeting client-imposed handover dates, and maintaining high-quality finishes in constrained environments — these are the skills that separate capable contractors from the rest.

Benchmark applies the same programme control and quality assurance to renovation projects as to new-build — with daily site reporting keeping clients informed of progress and allowing early intervention when constraints arise.

Scope Includes

  • Full building renovation and refurbishment
  • Commercial and residential interior fit-out
  • Heritage building restoration and adaptation
  • Partial-occupation renovation management
  • Defect rectification and planned maintenance works
