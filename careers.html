The Benchmark Pathway

Build a Career
in Construction

We are Zanzibar's most serious construction employer. We hire locally, train deliberately, and promote from within. Your career starts here.

Why We Train

The Construction Industry
Needs Zanzibari Leaders

Benchmark's directors came home from the UK and Russia with the skills and experience to build East Africa's most demanding projects. But skills imported from abroad are finite — and they do not belong to Zanzibar.

The Benchmark Pathway was created to change that. We believe that the next generation of construction engineers, site managers, and project directors in Zanzibar should be Zanzibari. Not brought in from elsewhere. Built here, from the ground up.

Every person who joins Benchmark — from site operative to graduate engineer — enters a structured progression pathway. We invest in training because we believe that the quality of the people on your site determines the quality of what gets built. And we intend to build the best.

Local Hiring preference — Zanzibar and Pemba Island first
5 Stages in the Benchmark career pathway
Real Projects — not simulations. You learn on live sites

Career Progression

The Five Stages

Every Benchmark employee is on the pathway. Where you enter depends on your experience and qualifications. Where you go is limited only by your commitment.

Stage
01

Site Operative

Entry point for anyone new to the industry. You learn site safety, material handling, basic construction methods, and what it means to work to a programme.

Stage
02

Skilled Tradesperson

Specialise in a core trade — concrete, masonry, formwork, finishing, or plant operation. Benchmark provides on-the-job training supported by structured skill assessment.

Stage
03

Site Supervisor

Manage a trade team, run daily toolbox talks, complete the site diary, and take ownership of your section of the programme. First management responsibility.

Stage
04

Site Engineer

Technical setting-out, reading and issuing drawings, programme management, quality control, and RAMS preparation. Benchmark supports qualification at this level.

Stage
05

Senior Engineer / PM

Full project management responsibility — client interface, design co-ordination, commercial management, and sub-contractor oversight. The Benchmark leadership tier.

What Benchmark Offers

More Than a Job

We are not building a workforce for today's projects. We are building the construction professionals Zanzibar will need for the next twenty years.

📐

On-Site Training

You learn on live Benchmark projects, managed by experienced site teams. Every day is a training day. No classroom simulations — real work, real consequences, real skills.

📋

Safety Training

Weekly toolbox talks. PPE provided on day one. RAMS documentation that you are involved in writing — not just signing. Safety is not optional at Benchmark.

📈

Structured Progression

Advancement at Benchmark is based on demonstrated competence — not seniority, not who you know. If you can do the job at the next level, we will put you in it.

🌍

International Standards

You will learn to work to a UK Tier 1 standard. The skills and discipline you develop with Benchmark are recognised and respected internationally.

🏝

Local First

Benchmark actively prefers to hire from Zanzibar and Pemba Island. We believe that the skills required to build Zanzibar should be owned by Zanzibaris.

💬

Bilingual Workplace

We operate in Swahili and English. Technical documentation is produced in both languages. You will develop professional competence in the construction vocabulary of both.
