Government Sector

Government and public-sector construction demands more than technical competence. It demands procurement compliance, transparent cost control, quality documentation that will survive audit, and the kind of professional relationships with ministry officials and project supervisors that are built on reliability over years — not promises over a tender submission.

Benchmark has been delivering government contracts since 2013. We hold ZPPDA registration W000367, our ZCR Class 1 certification qualifies us for all building and civil works, and our track record includes Zanzibar's most complex government healthcare facility — Micheweni District Hospital on Pemba Island.

Flagship Project

Micheweni
District Hospital

Our most technically demanding government project. Micheweni District Hospital on Pemba Island was delivered at a contract value of TZS 4.8 billion and now provides clinical services to 123,379 people across the district.

The project required full clinical specification management — infection control provisions, patient flow planning, medical services co-ordination, and all the documentation required by the Ministry of Health and ZPPDA. Delivered on programme. Delivered to specification.

Key Statistics

  • Contract value: TZS 4.8 billion
  • Population served: 123,379 people
  • Location: Micheweni, Pemba Island
  • Procured under ZPPDA framework
  • Full clinical and infection control specification
